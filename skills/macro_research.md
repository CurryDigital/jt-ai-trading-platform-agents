# Skill: Macro Research

## Key macro themes to track
- US trade policy: tariffs, sanctions, trade deals, reshoring
- Central banks: Fed, ECB, BOJ, PBOC rate decisions and guidance
- Geopolitical risk: conflict escalation/de-escalation, elections
- Commodity supply: OPEC, weather events, supply chain disruptions
- Crypto regulation: SEC actions, ETF approvals/rejections, stablecoin rules
- China data: PMI, exports, property market, stimulus measures

## Cross-asset impact patterns (learned from history)
- Trump tariff → industrials (XLI) ↓, gold (GLD) ↑, USD ↑
- Fed rate hike → REITs (VNQ) ↓, banks (XLF) ↑, bonds ↓
- OPEC cut → oil ↑, airlines (JETS) ↓, energy (XLE) ↑
- China PMI miss → copper ↓, AUD ↓, emerging markets ↓
- BTC ETF approval → BTC ↑, crypto mining stocks ↑
- Geopolitical escalation → VIX ↑, gold ↑, equities ↓

## How to log a macro event
Use the structured format in .learnings/MACRO_EVENTS.md.
Track prediction vs actual outcome to calibrate future confidence.

## Historical precedent requirement
Only generate trading hypotheses for HIGH confidence events where
similar past events produced measurable, consistent market impact.
Log the precedent: "Event X in {year} caused {asset} to move {%} in {N} days"
