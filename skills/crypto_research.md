# Skill: Crypto Research

## Alpha patterns for crypto markets
- 24/7 markets — no session boundaries, weekend effects different
- BTC dominance rotation: capital flows BTC → alts and back
- Funding rate signals: high funding = overlevered longs, reversal likely
- Halving cycle: historically bullish 6-12 months post-halving
- On-chain: exchange flows, whale movements (if data available)
- Correlation regime: BTC-equity correlation breaks during stress
- Regulatory events: SEC actions cause sharp temporary drops
